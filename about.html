Our Story

London is brilliant. But finding a decent room? That's less brilliant. We've been there — the endless Rightmove scrolling, the dodgy viewings, the shock of utility bills that somehow cost more than your rent.

Nook was born from a simple idea: what if renting a room in London was actually easy and enjoyable? What if you could move into a beautiful, furnished room where everything was sorted, and your flatmates were genuinely lovely people?

That's exactly what we've built. We handpick premium apartments in London's best neighbourhoods, furnish them beautifully, and fill them with a community of students, professionals, and creatives who make every day a little bit brighter.
